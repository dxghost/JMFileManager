JMFileManager

First please copy "avbin.dll" placed in "requirements" folder to "C:\Windows\System" and "C:\Windows\System32".

Modules Used for this project:
PyQt5, psutil, os, thearding, shutil, sys, PIL, subprocess, glob, mutagen, pyglet, win32api and zipfile.

Program Features:
New folder, Open by directory, Delete, Copy, Cut, Paste, Compress, Favorites, Rename, Back, Refresh, Tree view, Dedicated music player and Image Preview :)
